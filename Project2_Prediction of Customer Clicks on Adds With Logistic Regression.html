Problem statement¶

This project is to anaylze customer behaviour by predicting customer clicks on the Facebook advertisement, it goes from data visualization&cleaning to model traing&fitting and testing. The Customer dataset includes the following features:

Inputs:

  • Name
  • e-mail
  • Country
  • Time on Facebook
  • Estimated Salary (derived from other parameters)

Outputs:

  • Click (1: customer clicked on Ad, 0: Customer did not click on the Ad)
